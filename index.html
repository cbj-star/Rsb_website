We fight together

On 17 Feb 2021, our CSR colleague , Mr.Chetan Kusalkar, organised the interactive Group Discussion session for Grade 11th and 12th. Total 9 students were from Mangalwar Peth, Pune, Slum area. Session was in Indian Gurukool style. The subject of discussion wasImportance of 11th 12th and how it will help to pursue our career* Well done. Cheers.

We fight together

Our Mr Sachikanta Tripathi, organized Homeopathy Health Camp @ Badalgoda , Dumuria, deep rural Jharkhand, today, 16.2.21. This is an on going health camp every month. This is another CSR initiative for under privileged rural folks from RSB Foundation.Good job done by our colleague.

June 10,2020 Aurangabad

WE care about others

Our Mr Chetan Kusalkar conducted today, 16.2.21, interactive session to under privileged children of slum at Janata Vashat at Paravati, Pune, on "Importance of Time Management in Academics" and related knitty gritty of making a time table in PDCA cycle.Total of 40 students attended. Another E- voluntary initiative from RSB Foundation for under privileged.
